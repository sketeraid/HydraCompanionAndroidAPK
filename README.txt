**Hydra Companion (Android)**

Hydra Companion is a Kotlin‑built Android app that stores data locally and helps RAID: Shadow Legends players track pity, manage shard inventory, and log every pull through a clean, intuitive interface.

---

**Features**

- Track pity for Ancient, Void, Sacred, and Primal shards  
- Manage shard inventory with automatic updates  
- Log every pull with rarity detection and history  
- Local data storage — nothing leaves your device  
- Fast, lightweight, and built for clarity

---

**Requirements**

**Requires Android 12 (Snow Cone) or higher**

---

**Installation**

1. Download the latest `app-release.apk` from the **Releases** section.  
2. Install the APK on your Android device.  
3. Open Hydra Companion and start tracking your pulls.
